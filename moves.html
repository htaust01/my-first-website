The Moves

These are the instructions that are used for the moves. We label each of the faces as Right (R), Left(L), Up(U), Down(D), Front(F), and Back(B). You turn the face clockwise 90 degrees as if you were looking directly at the face. If there is an accent mark folling the letter(as in R`) then you turn that face counter-clockwise 90 degrees. If the letter is followed by a 2 (as in R2) you rotate the face 180 degrees (either clockwise or counter-clockwise, they are the same in this case.)
